LovoRise Privacy Policy

Hey, there! Welcome to the LovoRise Privacy Policy.

We understand that reading a privacy policy is not everyone's idea of a good time, but hear us out. We worked hard to make our policy as clear and entertaining as possible so that you would actually read it! Consider us your digital wingmate, here to walk you through the details of what data we gather, why we collect it, and how we utilise it. So get cosy, take your favourite beverage, and let's explore the world of personal data together.

This Privacy Policy applies from July, 2024

1. Where This Privacy Policy Applies

This Privacy Policy applies to websites, apps, events, and other services we operate under the LovoRise brand. Whether you're searching for your soulmate, joining us at one of our events, or using any of our other awesome services, this Policy has got you covered. For simplicity, we just refer to all of these as our “service” in this Privacy Policy.

If for some reason, one of our services requires its own separate privacy policy, then that policy will be made available to you and that policy—not this Privacy Policy—will apply.

2. Data Collector

The data collected is processed by LovoRise Ltd. , a company registered under number 15814939 in the London Trade and Companies Register. The company's headquarters are located at 71-75 Shelton Street, London WC2H 9JQ, United Kingdom.

3. Data We Collect

We can't help you develop meaningful connections without you providing some information about yourself, like basic profile details and the types of people you’d like to meet. Using our service also generates some information, such as when you logged in and how you used the service.

Data You Give Us

  • Account Data: When you create an account, you provide basic information like email address, and date of birth.
  • Profile Data: When you complete your profile, you share additional details like your gender, interests, preferences, and approximate location.
  • Content: Photos, videos, audio, text, and other content you upload, including chats with other members.
  • Purchase Data: Details of transactions you make, such as what you purchased and when.
  • Marketing, Survey, and Research Data: Information you provide when you participate in surveys, focus groups, promotions, events, or contests.
  • Customer Support Data: Information you provide when you contact our support team. Data Generated or Automatically Collected
  • Usage Data: Information about your activity on the service, like when you logged in, features used, and interactions with others.
  • Technical Data: Information from your device, such as IP address, device ID and type, app settings, app crashes, and identifiers associated with cookies or other technologies.
  • Geolocation Data: Your location data if you give us permission.

4. Why and How We Use Your Data

We process your data to provide and improve our service, connect you with other members, personalize your experience, ensure safety and security, and show relevant ads. Here are more detailed explanations:

Purposes for Processing Your Data

  • To enable you to use our service: Creating and maintaining your account, recommending other members, organizing sweepstakes, responding to requests, troubleshooting issues, and processing applications for premiere/VIP services.
  • To enable your purchases: Processing payments and offering discounts and promotions.
  • To operate advertising and marketing campaigns: Performing and measuring effectiveness of ads and marketing campaigns.
  • To improve our service and create new features: Conducting research, analyzing service use, reviewing customer interactions, and developing new features.
  • To keep you and other members safe: Preventing and detecting violations of our Terms, fraud, and unauthorized activities, verifying identity, securing payments, and supporting investigations.
  • To comply with applicable law: Preserving data, supporting investigations, responding to legal requests, and establishing, exercising, or defending legal claims.

5. Your Rights

You have rights regarding your data, such as the right to access, rectify, delete, object to, or restrict processing of your data. You can exercise these rights through your account settings or by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Access, Portability, or To Know: Right to be informed of the personal data we process about you and ask for a copy of it.
  • Rectification or Correction: Right to amend or update your personal data where it's inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion or Erasure: Right to delete personal data.
  • Objection (Opt-out) or Restriction: Right to object to us processing personal data or to request that we stop processing personal data.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Right to withdraw a consent you've given us to process your personal data for a specific purpose.

6. What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • All your personal information, photos, and content associated with your account will be permanently deleted.
  • This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, we cannot recover any deleted data.
  • Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory reasons as required by law.

Need Help?
If you have deleted your account but wish to ensure all your personal data is removed, please go to this Contact Us link to request for it.

7. How Long We Retain Your Data

We keep your personal data only as long as necessary for legitimate business reasons and as permitted by applicable law. We have a retention policy to ensure we do not retain data longer than necessary.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is restricted to individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not permit individuals under the age of 18 on our platform. If you suspect that a member is under the age of 18, please use the reporting mechanism available on the service.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

This policy may change over time. We will notify you before material changes take effect so that you have time to review the changes.
