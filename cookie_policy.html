Cookie Policy

At LovoRise, transparency about our data collection and processing practices is our priority. This page explains our use of cookies and how you can manage them.

Already familiar with cookies and want to adjust your settings?
No problem! Head to your account settings in the app to adjust your privacy preferences.

Want to learn more about cookies and our use of them?
Keep reading for more information. Note: This Cookie Policy specifically addresses our use of cookies. For details on how we process your personal information beyond cookies, please read our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your web browser or device's memory. They typically contain the domain name (internet location) from which the cookie originated, the cookie's expiration date, and a randomly generated unique identifier. Cookies may also include information about your device, such as user settings, browsing history, and activities conducted while using our services.

Are there different types of cookies?

First-party and third-party cookies

  • First-party cookies are placed directly by us on your device. We use them to adapt our website to your browser's language preferences and to better understand how you use our website.
  • Third-party cookies are placed by our partners and service providers. You can learn more about these partners and service providers via our website and in-app consent management tools.

Session and persistent cookies

  • Session cookies last only until you close your browser. We use them to learn more about your use of our website during a single browser session and to help you use our website more efficiently.
  • Persistent cookies remain on your device beyond the current session. They help you quickly sign in to our website again and are also used for analytical purposes.

What do we use cookies for?

Like other online service providers, we use cookies to deliver, secure, and enhance our services. This includes remembering your preferences, recognizing you when you visit our website, measuring the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns, and personalizing ads to suit your interests. To achieve these goals, we may also link cookie information with other personal data we have about you. When using our website, you are in control and may refuse to accept Cookies at any time by managing your preferences.

OpenStreetMap Cookies

Some features of our website and certain LovoRise services rely on OpenStreetMap Cookies, which will be stored on your device. By browsing this website and using services that depend on OpenStreetMap Cookies, you consent to the storage, collection, access, usage, and sharing of such cookies by OpenStreetMap. OpenStreetMap manages the information and your choices related to OpenStreetMap Cookies through an interface separate from your browser. For more information, please visit OpenStreetMap's Cookie Policy.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics, a service provided by Google that employs cookies and other data collection technologies to gather information about your use of our website and services to report on website trends.
For details on how Google collects and processes data, please visit Google's Partner Sites Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by downloading the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on and opt out of Google's ad personalization at Google Ads Settings.
