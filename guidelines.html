LovoRise Guidelines

Welcome to LovoRise! We aim to create a safe, respectful, and enjoyable community for everyone. To ensure this, we have established the following guidelines. By using our app, you agree to adhere to these principles.

1. Profile Guidelines

  • Accurate Representation: Ensure your profile accurately represents who you are. Use recent photos and truthful descriptions.
  • Appropriate Photos: Upload clear, high-quality photos of yourself. Avoid nudity, sexually explicit content, or any images that may be considered offensive.
  • Respectful Language: Use respectful and appropriate language in your profile. Avoid vulgar, offensive, or discriminatory language.
  • Genuine Intentions: Profiles created with the intention to deceive, scam, or harm others are strictly prohibited.
  • No Spam or Advertising: Do not use your profile to advertise products, services, or other websites. Spamming is not tolerated

2. Content Conduct Guidelines

  • Original content. Post original content and respect the intellectual property rights of others.
  • Positive and Encouraging: Share positive, uplifting content that contributes to a supportive community environment
  • No Inappropriate Content: Avoid posting or sharing content that is violent, sexually explicit, or otherwise inappropriate for a diverse community.
  • Constructive Feedback: If offering feedback or critique, do so constructively and with respect for the other person's feelings and efforts.
  • No Misleading Content: Do not post or share false information, rumors, or content designed to mislead or deceive others.

3. Respect and Kindness

  • Treat others with respect. Always be kind, considerate, and polite in your interactions.
  • No harassment or hate speech. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or hate speech based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic is strictly prohibited.

4. Honesty and Integrity

  • Be honest. Present yourself truthfully. Do not impersonate others or provide misleading information about yourself.
  • No catfishing. Creating fake profiles or pretending to be someone you are not is unacceptable.
  • Safety First
  • Protect your personal information. Never share personal information like your address, financial details, or social security number with people you just met online.
  • Report suspicious activity. If you encounter someone who seems suspicious or is making you uncomfortable, report them to our support team immediately.
  • Consensual Interactions
  • Consent is key. Always ensure that your interactions are consensual. Respect boundaries and no means no.
  • No unsolicited content. Do not send unsolicited explicit content or make unwelcome advances.

5. Inclusive Environment

Celebrate diversity. We welcome people of all backgrounds and strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued.

No discrimination. Discriminatory behavior or content is not tolerated.

6. Positive Engagement

Our service is restricted to individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not permit individuals under the age of 18 on our platform. If you suspect that a member is under the age of 18, please use the reporting mechanism available on the service.

7. Community and Collaboration

Support one another. Engage in the community by supporting others and contributing positively.
Report violations. Help us maintain a safe space by reporting any violations of these guidelines.

8. Privacy and Confidentiality

Respect privacy. Do not share private conversations or personal information of others without their consent.
Confidentiality matters. Keep sensitive information shared with you confidential.

9. Compliance with Laws

Follow the law. Ensure that your actions on the app comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
No illegal activities. Engaging in or promoting illegal activities is strictly prohibited.

10. Consequences of Violating Guidelines

Violations of these guidelines may result in actions such as warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the app. Our support team reserves the right to review and take appropriate action on any reported behavior.
