Our Story

LovoRise was launched in 2024 by LovoRise Ltd., headquartered at London. The founders recognized a growing need for a dating app that goes beyond superficial interactions and truly connects people based on deeper compatibility and shared values.
In a world where digital connections often feel fleeting and impersonal, LovoRise was created to make dating more meaningful. By leveraging advanced algorithms and comprehensive people profiles, the app ensures that matches are based on genuine compatibility. Safety is a top priority, with features like verified profiles and secure messaging to create a trustworthy environment for people.

Why choose a dating app like LovoRise?

LovoRise differentiates itself by focusing on deeper, more authentic connections, people safety, and a supportive, inclusive community. If you're looking for a dating app that prioritizes meaningful relationships and provides a secure environment, LovoRise is a great choice. By combining comprehensive matching algorithms, robust safety features, and engaging interactive tools, LovoRise offers a refreshing alternative for those seeking genuine and lasting connections in the digital dating world.
